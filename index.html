Company Values

For a small billboard business like Yamashita Media Group, our company values focus on qualities that set it apart and align with both customer satisfaction and operational integrity, prioritizing client satisfaction by offering customized ad solutions that meet the unique needs of each business, ensuring high visibility and impactful messaging. We uphold honest communication with clients regarding pricing, contract terms, and ad performance, ensuring that expectations are clearly set and met.

Company Summary

We specialise in wooden frame classic style billboards on building walls. Our approach is to be very negotiable with clients and to strive for sites to be constantly running advertisements. We operate predominantly in the Auckland area and would be happy for people with sites in seek of extra cashflow or companies looking for advertising to reach out with the contact details at the bottom of this page.
